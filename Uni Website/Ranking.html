Ranking

Titles are awared to players depending on their rating and how well they have performed.

Titles are only awared in ranked matches when certain milestones are hit, for example reaching a rating of 2200 will give you the Candiate Master Title.

However, what title you gain at each rating may differ depending on what chess governing body you play under.
