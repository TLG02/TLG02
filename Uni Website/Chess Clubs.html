Chess Clubs

Chess Clubs are groups of people that are united by the interest of playing chess.

Every Chess Club is different. A example of how this can vary, is how long the club has been established for and how many members it has.

Apart from playing the game in general, chess clubs can have leagues and offer personal training in the game.

Outside of a Chess Club playing over the broad and playing online.
Chess Clubs that have a strong following both on the interent and offline are the clubs that are the most popular.
